# 🎮 Echoes of Regret

**Echoes of Regret** is an emotionally-driven 2D narrative game that blends storytelling with gameplay. Players experience Shiv's journey through trauma, addiction, and ultimately, redemption — each phase represented through symbolic mini-games and an emotional climax.

🔗 [Play on Itch.io](https://chinmaybiswas475.itch.io/echoes-of-regret)  
📦 [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/Chinmay-biswas/Echoes-of-Regret)

---

## 🧠 Game Concept

Players explore Shiv’s subconscious, reliving key moments in his life and helping him find meaning through challenges that reflect his inner turmoil.

### 🕹️ Game Structure

- **🎹 The Piano** – A rhythm mini-game depicting Shiv’s struggle to reconnect with his musical self.
- **🃏 Cards** – A memory game capturing his love and loss with Maya.
- **🎰 Gambling** – A betting sequence showing his addiction and helplessness.
- **💡 Light** – Puzzle-solving in darkness symbolizing gradual mental clarity.
- **🌀 Awakening** – The final symbolic showdown where Shiv chooses between despair or redemption.
